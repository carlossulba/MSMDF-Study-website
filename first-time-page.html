Understanding the Experiment

The study involves 10 data collection sessions, each lasting approximately 2 minutes. You can complete these sessions whenever it's convenient for you.

During each data collection session, you will follow these steps:

  • Step 1: Hold your phone still in your hand for 10 seconds.
  • Step 2: Place your phone on a flat surface for 10 seconds.
  • Step 3: Repeat Steps 1 and 2, this time the website will play inaudible audio.
  • Step 4: Walk 10 steps in a straight, stop, walk 10 steps in a different direction.

Please follow these steps carefully to ensure accurate data collection. Your participation in all 10 data collection sessions is crucial for the success of this study.
