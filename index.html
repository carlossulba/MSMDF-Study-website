About the Study

Welcome(Hola), and thank you for considering participating in our study! Your involvement is crucial in helping us explore the privacy implications of motion sensors in mobile devices.

Motion sensors, such as accelerometers and gyroscopes, play an important role in how mobile devices interact with the world. However, these sensors can also be used to track devices through a technique known as Motion Sensor-based Mobile Device Fingerprinting. This study aims to evaluate and develop countermeasures against this.
