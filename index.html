Mobile Device Privacy Defenses Study

Welcome to our motion sensor data collection website!

About the Experiment

Motion sensors in mobile devices can be used to digitally track devices. This technique is known as Motion Sensor-based Mobile Device Fingerprinting. This experiment aims to evaluate countermeasures against it.

This experiment collects data from the accelerometer and gyroscope. Your participation will contribute to a better understanding of how to protect user privacy in mobile environments.

Please watch the video below for a detailed explanation of the steps you'll need to follow during each data collection session. There are 8 sessions, each lasting around 2 minutes.

Privacy Assurance

All data collected in this study will be anonymized and used solely for research purposes. Only data from the motion sensors will be collected. No personal or device identifiers will be recorded.
